Enrolment

YOUNG STAR - English Kindergarten enrolls children between the ages of 2 and 6 years old. Your child can stay for a full day, basic session), half day (up to 4 hours), or on an hourly basis.

Registration

When the child is enrolled for the first time, a Registration fee is charged once:

  • 90 EUR - for half day care service
  • 150 EUR - for basicl day care service
  • 180 EUR - for full day care service.

Payment of the Registration fee guarantees the booking of a place in YOUNG STAR.

Fees

2 years old*

  • Full time day care service (7:30-17:00) - 1000 EUR
  • Basic day care service (7:30-14:30) - 900 EUR
  • Half time day care service (8:00-12:00) or (13:00-17:00) - 700 EUR.

*(KELA compensates: 663-64-869,66)

3 -6 yeras old*

  • Full time day care service (7:30-17:00) - 830 EUR
  • Basic day care service (7:30-14:30) - 750 EUR
  • Half time day care service (8:00-12) or (13:00-17:00) - 500 EUR.

*(KELA compensates: 493,64-699,64)

The kindergartens fees are paid monthly. A large portion of this fee is compensated by KELA.

The monthly fee is a set amount that does not change based on attendance.
The enrolment coverrs 12 months period with a guaranteed place for extended enrolment for already enroled children.
For each child enrolled in day care services at YOUNG STAR, the same monthly fee has to be paid for July (summer holiday). All fees are non-refundable.
