What is my philosophy?

I totally concur with the Russian web-developer and blogger Artemy Lebedev in his philosophy about the purpose of web-design:

Design is not something to discuss, it is created to fulfil the task. The only thing that can be discussed is whether the task has or has not been achieved.

Artemy Lebedev

I am very demanding towards myself. I believe that the good work is the best that I can contribute to the world. This is where I really start respecting myself - when I am working, and see the outcome. This moments I really like myself, which does not happen very frequently.
